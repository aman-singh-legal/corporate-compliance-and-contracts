## 4. FINANCIAL ARRANGEMENTS
Unless otherwise agreed in writing, each Party shall bear its own costs and expenses incurred in connection with this MoU.  
If any financial transactions, joint funding, or grants are involved, they shall be governed by a separate written agreement specifying budgets, payment terms, and reporting mechanisms.

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS (IPR)
1. Each Party shall retain ownership of its pre-existing intellectual property.  
2. Any jointly developed intellectual property resulting from collaborative activities shall be governed by a separate **Joint IP Agreement** or **Project Contract** specifying ownership, usage rights, and commercial terms.  
3. Neither Party shall use the other Party’s name, logo, or trademarks without prior written consent.

---

## 6. CONFIDENTIALITY
1. Both Parties agree to treat as confidential all non-public information disclosed during the course of collaboration.  
2. The receiving Party shall not disclose such information to third parties without the written consent of the disclosing Party.  
3. The confidentiality obligations shall remain valid for **[2–3 years]** after termination of this MoU.  
4. Information that is publicly available or obtained independently shall not be subject to this clause.

## 7. DURATION AND TERMINATION
1. This MoU shall commence on the **Effective Date** and remain in force for a period of **[1 / 2 / 3 years]**, unless terminated earlier.

3. Termination of this MoU shall not affect the completion of any ongoing projects, unless agreed otherwise.  
4. Obligations concerning confidentiality, intellectual property, and dispute resolution shall survive termination.

## 8. AMENDMENTS
This MoU may be amended or modified only by mutual written agreement signed by authorized representatives of both Parties.
